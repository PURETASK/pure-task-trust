64 · Background-checked trust page
← Trust at PureTask
Every cleaner is verified.

Background check, identity verification, ongoing performance scoring. We don't take shortcuts on who you let into your home.

What we verify
Identity verified
Government ID confirmed via Stripe Identity. Selfie matched to ID.
Background check passed
National criminal database, sex offender registry, global watchlists. Run by Checkr. Renews every 12 months.
Performance scored daily
On-time arrival, photo compliance, customer ratings tracked continuously. Cleaners falling below standard pause from new bookings.
GPS-verified arrivals
Cleaners check in via geo-fence. You see exact arrival and departure times.
What we don't do
We don't read your messages. We don't track cleaners' GPS during the cleaning itself. We don't share your address with cleaners we haven't matched you with. We don't hide our verification process — every step above is auditable.

Why it exists
A cleaner who's great in one neighborhood may not be in another — different home types, different expectations, different commute patterns. ZIP-locked badges reward sustained local excellence rather than a single overall rating.
