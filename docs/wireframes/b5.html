29 · Privacy · photo policy + data controls
← Privacy
Photos protect both you and your cleaner. They're encrypted, kept 90 days, then automatically deleted. You control what gets photographed.
Photo policy
Default · all rooms photographed
Cleaner takes wide-angle before/after photos of every room cleaned. No close-ups of personal items.
●
Skip specific rooms
Tell cleaners to skip photos in named rooms. Other rooms still photographed for dispute protection.
○
Skip all photos · with dispute waiver
Without photo evidence, dispute resolution relies on clock-in/out times and any photos you provide yourself.
○
Required acknowledgment: "I waive the right to dispute solely on cleaning quality without providing my own photo evidence."
How photos are stored
✓ Encrypted at rest, only visible to you, your cleaner, and dispute reviewers
✓ Automatically deleted 90 days after each booking
✓ Never used for marketing, sold, or shared with third parties
✓ Never used to train AI/ML systems

Batch 5E — Insurance Verified upload flow (3 states · earns badge)
32a · Insurance · not uploaded · the pitch
← Insurance Verified
🛡
Earn the Insurance Verified badge
Upload proof of personal liability insurance. Customers can filter for insured cleaners — it's a real competitive advantage.
Why this matters
✓ Customer trust signal. Insured cleaners earn more bookings — verified data from similar marketplaces.
✓ Financial protection. If you damage something, your insurance handles it instead of you paying out of pocket.
✓ Visible badge on your profile. Shows up next to your tier in search results and your full profile.
Optional, not required. You can clean on PureTask without insurance. The badge is opt-in for cleaners who carry coverage.
Need insurance? · approximate costs
Personal liability insurance for residential cleaners typically runs $300–500/year through providers like:
Insureon · Hiscox · Next Insurance
PureTask isn't affiliated with any provider. Compare and choose what works for you.
What you'll need to upload
· Certificate of Insurance (COI) PDF or photo
· Must show your name, policy number, expiration date
· Minimum coverage: $100,000 per occurrence
· Verification takes 1–2 business days
Stored encrypted · only used for verification
