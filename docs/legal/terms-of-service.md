## 5. Bookings, Pricing, and Credits

### 5.1 Booking Process
A Client submits a job request describing the property, scope of work, and preferred timing. Eligible Cleaners receive the offer and may accept it. Upon acceptance, an enforceable service contract is formed directly between the Client and the Cleaner, subject to these Terms.

### 5.2 Credits
The Platform uses an internal **Credits** system. Credits are a stored-value mechanism used to pay for cleaning services and related fees on the Platform. Credits:

- Are denominated in U.S. dollars at a fixed conversion rate established by PureTask;
- Have **no cash value** outside the Platform except to the extent PureTask permits a payout in accordance with these Terms;
- Are **non-transferable** between Users;
- May expire under conditions disclosed at the time of purchase or grant; and
- Are not deposits, bank accounts, or stored-value instruments under the Electronic Fund Transfer Act, and PureTask is not a bank.

### 5.3 Pricing
Cleaner rates are set by Cleaners within Platform-defined floors and ceilings. PureTask may add **platform fees**, **rush fees**, **same-day booking fees**, **cancellation fees**, **trust and safety fees**, **processing fees**, and **taxes** to the displayed price. All fees are disclosed before checkout.

### 5.4 Escrow and Release
When a Client books a job, the Credit amount is **held in escrow**. Upon job completion, Credits enter a **24-hour Review Window** during which the Client may approve, dispute, or request changes. If no action is taken within 24 hours, the escrow **auto-releases** to the Cleaner. Approving early immediately releases payment. Filing a dispute pauses release pending resolution.

### 5.5 Tipping
Clients may add a tip post-completion. Tips are paid in full to the Cleaner (less applicable processing fees) and are not subject to PureTask's commission.

## 6. Cancellations, Refunds, and No-Shows

Cancellations are governed by our [Cancellation Policy](/cancellation-policy), which is incorporated by reference. In summary:

- **6+ hours' notice:** Free cancellation.
- **2–6 hours' notice:** 50% cancellation fee.
- **Under 2 hours' notice:** 100% cancellation fee.
- **No-show by Cleaner:** Client receives a full refund of held Credits and the Cleaner's reliability score is adjusted.
- **No-show by Client:** Cleaner is compensated for the booked time per the policy.

Refunds, where granted, are returned to the Client's Credit balance. Cash refunds to the original payment method are issued at PureTask's discretion and may take 5–10 business days.
