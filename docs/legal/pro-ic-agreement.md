## 1. Recitals

A. PureTask operates a technology platform (the "**Platform**") that helps Clients discover, schedule, communicate with, and pay independent cleaning professionals.

B. Pro is an **independent business** engaged in the trade, occupation, or business of providing cleaning services to the public, separate and apart from PureTask. Pro represents that Pro holds or will hold **at least one** of the following at all times during the term of this Agreement: a valid business license; a valid Employer Identification Number (EIN); or a valid Certificate of Insurance evidencing Commercial General Liability coverage of at least **\$300,000 per occurrence and \$1,000,000 aggregate**, naming PureTask LLC as an additional insured.

C. Pro desires to use the Platform to discover and book cleaning service opportunities with Clients, and PureTask is willing to make the Platform available to Pro, subject to this Agreement.

D. The parties intend their relationship to be that of two **independent businesses**. Pro is **not** an employee, joint venturer, agent, partner, or franchisee of PureTask. PureTask is **not** an employer, principal, joint venturer, or franchisor of Pro.

## 2. Definitions

- **"Booking"** means a service engagement between a Client and Pro initiated and confirmed through the Platform.
- **"Client"** means an end user of the Platform who books cleaning services.
- **"Platform Fee"** means the flat per-Booking fee that PureTask charges the Pro as described in Section 7.
- **"Pro Services"** means the cleaning services that Pro provides directly to a Client under a Pro--Client service contract.
- **"Service Area"** means the geographic area in which Pro chooses to make Pro Services available, within the Operating States of California, Texas, and Florida.

## 3. Independent Contractor Status (Material Term)

The parties expressly intend and agree that Pro is an **independent contractor**, not an employee of PureTask.

### 3.1 No Employment Relationship

This Agreement does not create:

- An employer--employee relationship;
- An agency relationship (except for the limited "agent of the payee" authority described in Section 8);
- A partnership, joint venture, or franchise;
- A fiduciary relationship; or
- Any obligation by PureTask to provide Pro with work, hours, or income of any kind.

### 3.2 Pro's Acknowledgment

Pro acknowledges that, as an independent contractor:

- Pro is **not entitled** to any employee benefits from PureTask, including health insurance, retirement contributions, paid time off, sick leave, workers' compensation coverage (PureTask does not provide it; Pro is responsible for obtaining it where required by law), unemployment insurance, or disability benefits.
- PureTask **will not withhold** any federal, state, or local income, payroll, Social Security, or Medicare taxes from amounts payable to Pro. Pro is solely responsible for all such taxes.
- PureTask **will not provide** Pro with tools, supplies, equipment, transportation, uniforms, or training, except for use of the Platform itself.
- Pro is **not eligible** for unemployment benefits based on work performed through the Platform.

### 3.3 ABC Test Acknowledgment (California)

For purposes of California Labor Code § 2775 et seq. (the "ABC Test"), the parties agree and acknowledge:

- **Prong A (No control over performance):** Pro is free from PureTask's control and direction in the performance of Pro Services, both under this Agreement and in fact, as further set forth in Section 4.
- **Prong B (Outside usual course of business):** PureTask's usual course of business is the development and operation of a marketplace technology platform, payment-processing facilitation, dispute mediation, and trust-and-safety services. PureTask is **not** in the business of providing cleaning services. Pro performs cleaning services, which are outside PureTask's usual course of business.
- **Prong C (Independently established trade):** Pro is customarily engaged in an independently established trade, occupation, or business of the same nature as the Pro Services, as evidenced by Pro's business license, EIN, or insurance, and Pro's right to provide cleaning services through other platforms, agencies, or directly to clients.

### 3.4 Multi-State Acknowledgment

For purposes of **Texas Labor Code § 201.041**, **Florida Statutes § 440.02(15)(d)**, and the multi-factor common-law and IRS tests applicable in those states, the parties agree that the substance of their relationship --- including the freedoms, responsibilities, and economic indicia set forth in this Agreement --- supports independent contractor status.

## 4. Pro's Rights, Freedoms, and Control

### 4.1 Schedule and Availability

Pro chooses when to be available on the Platform, when to log in or log off, which days and hours to work, and how many Bookings to accept. No minimum availability, jobs, or login duration is required.

### 4.2 Rates

Pro sets Pro's own rates, subject only to a published maximum-rate ceiling. PureTask does **not** set a minimum rate, wage floor, or required surcharge.

### 4.3 Accept or Decline Without Penalty

Pro may accept or decline any Booking offer for any lawful reason. Acceptance or decline rate will **not** be used to suspend or penalize the account.

### 4.4 Service Area

Pro defines Pro's own Service Area within the Operating States. PureTask does not require any particular area.

### 4.5 Methods, Tools, Supplies, Vehicles, Attire

Pro provides Pro's own equipment, supplies, transportation, and attire. PureTask does **not** require any brand, method, uniform, or vehicle. Pro chooses order, pace, and technique.

### 4.6 Work for Others

Pro may, and is encouraged to, work outside the Platform, including for competitors (Handy, TaskRabbit, Thumbtack, NextDoor, direct clients, etc.). No exclusivity.

### 4.7 Subcontracting and Substitution

Pro may subcontract or substitute a qualified replacement to perform any Booking, provided the substitute:

- Is registered on the Platform;
- Has completed PureTask's identity and background screening;
- Is **at least 18 years of age**; and
- The Client is notified through the Platform.

Pro remains responsible for the substitute's performance.

### 4.8 No Required Training

PureTask does not require Pro to complete cleaning training, attend meetings, or follow any cleaning methodology. Optional resources are informational only.

### 4.9 No Exclusive Dealing

Pro is not required to work exclusively for PureTask. No non-compete obligation.

## 7. Compensation and Platform Fees

### 7.1 Pro's Compensation

Pro is compensated by the **Client** directly for Pro Services, in the amount Pro sets (subject to the maximum-rate ceiling). PureTask is not the source of Pro's compensation.

### 7.2 PureTask's Platform Fee

Pro agrees to pay PureTask a **flat per-Booking Platform Fee**, disclosed in the Pro dashboard, in exchange for use of the Platform's technology, matching, payment facilitation, dispute mediation, and trust-and-safety services. The Platform Fee is deducted from the Client's payment before remaining funds are released to Pro's connected Stripe Express account.

PureTask may change the Platform Fee on no less than **30 days'** advance notice. Continued use of the Platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance. Pro may terminate this Agreement (Section 14) if Pro does not agree.

### 7.3 No Commission on Tips

Tips paid by Clients are paid in full to Pro, less only Stripe's processing fee on the tip itself. PureTask retains no portion.

### 7.4 Pass-Through Fees

PureTask may collect and remit pass-through fees (rush, same-day, trust-and-safety, cancellation, taxes) from the Client. These are not part of Pro's compensation.

Pro authorizes PureTask to receive payments on Pro's behalf, deduct the Platform Fee and pass-through fees, and direct Stripe to remit the balance to Pro's connected account.
