# PureTask — Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date:** May 17, 2026
**Version:** 2.0

This Cancellation Policy is part of and incorporated by reference into the **PureTask [Terms of Service](/legal/terms)**. It explains how cancellations, modifications, no-shows, partial completions, and refunds work on the PureTask Platform operated by **PureTask LLC**.

By booking through PureTask, you agree to this policy. All cancellation fees are disclosed in the booking flow **before** payment is authorized, consistent with **California Business & Professions Code § 17500.5** (Honest Pricing Law), the **Texas Deceptive Trade Practices Act**, and the **Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act**.

---

## 1. Client Cancellations (One-Time Bookings)

You may cancel a Booking through the Platform. Fees depend on how much notice you give before the **scheduled start time**:

| Notice Before Start Time | Cancellation Fee |
| --- | --- |
| **6 or more hours** | **No fee.** Full refund to original payment method. |
| **2 to less than 6 hours** | **50% of the Booking total** (excluding tip). Remaining 50% refunded. |
| **Less than 2 hours** | **100% of the Booking total** (excluding tip). No refund. |
| **No-show by Client at scheduled time** | **100% of the Booking total** (excluding tip). No refund. |

The “**Booking total**” means the agreed service price plus any rush, same-day, or trust-and-safety fees, but does **not** include voluntary tips (which are added post-completion only).

---

## 2. Booking Modifications (Not a Full Cancellation)

If you need to change a Booking (different date/time, different scope) without canceling outright, you may modify the Booking through the Platform:

| Modification Type | Treatment |
| --- | --- |
| **Reschedule, 6+ hours notice** | Free reschedule, subject to Pro availability. |
| **Reschedule, less than 6 hours notice** | Treated as a cancellation per Section 1, plus a new Booking is required. |
| **Increase scope** | Pro must agree; additional charge processed via the Platform. |
| **Decrease scope** | Pro must agree; partial refund of difference, if any, returned to original payment method. |
| **Change of address** | Treated as a cancellation if the new address is outside the Pro’s Service Area. |

---

## 3. Recurring Bookings

For Clients enrolled in **recurring Bookings** (weekly, biweekly, monthly):

- Each instance is subject to the cancellation rules in Section 1, applied to that instance’s scheduled start time.
- **Cancel the entire recurring schedule**: at any time, with no fee for future instances. Past or in-progress instances are governed by Section 1.
- **Pro changes for recurring Bookings**: a Pro may decline to continue a recurring schedule; PureTask will offer to match the Client with a substitute Pro.

---

## 4. Pro Cancellations

| Event | Consequence |
| --- | --- |
| **Pro cancels 6+ hours before start** | Client receives full refund and free re-booking with another Pro. Logged on Reliability Indicator. |
| **Pro cancels less than 6 hours before start** | Full refund + free re-booking + one-time PureTask service credit toward a future Booking. Logged on Reliability Indicator. |
| **Pro no-show (does not arrive within 30 minutes of scheduled start)** | Full refund. Logged on Reliability Indicator. Repeated no-shows may result in suspension. |

Pros are independent contractors and have the right to decline or cancel Bookings. Repeated last-minute cancellations or no-shows may, however, result in suspension under the [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/aup) for failure to perform contractual commitments to Clients.

---

## 5. Partial Completion

If a Pro completes a portion of a Booking but cannot complete the full scope (e.g., unexpected medical issue, equipment failure, undisclosed hazard discovered on-site):

| Scenario | Treatment |
| --- | --- |
| **Pro completes ≥75% of scope** | Full payment, with Client option to file a dispute for partial credit if quality concerns exist. |
| **Pro completes 25–75% of scope** | Pro paid pro-rata; remaining balance refunded to Client. |
| **Pro completes <25% of scope** | Pro paid the **trip fee** (50% of Booking total if attributable to undisclosed property conditions per Section 6; otherwise refunded in full). |

PureTask may exercise reasonable discretion in evaluating partial completion based on photos, timer data, and User reports.

---

## 6. Cancellations Due to Safety, Hazard, or Misrepresentation

If a Pro arrives and reasonably determines that:

- The property poses an undisclosed safety hazard (biohazards, unsecured firearms, aggressive animals, structural risks);
- The actual property condition is materially different from the booking description (significantly larger, in significantly worse condition);
- The Client is intoxicated, hostile, or has made the workspace unsafe;
- The Client has requested services outside the agreed scope, or that violate the AUP, or that would be illegal,

the Pro may cancel the Booking on-site without performing services. In such cases:

- The Client is charged a **trip fee of 50% of the Booking total** to compensate the Pro for time and transportation;
- The remaining 50% is refunded; and
- The incident is logged.

A Client who repeatedly misrepresents property conditions may be suspended.

---

## 7. Client-Initiated Mid-Job Termination

If a Client requests the Pro to stop work mid-Booking:

| Reason | Treatment |
| --- | --- |
| **Pro misconduct (verified)** | Full refund; Pro may face suspension; incident reviewed by PureTask. |
| **Client change of mind** | Pro paid pro-rata for time worked, minimum 50% of Booking total; remainder refunded. |
| **Emergency (medical, family, evacuation)** | PureTask may at its discretion waive the fee under Section 8. |

---

## 8. Cancellation Due to Emergencies and Force Majeure

If a Booking cannot be performed due to events beyond either party’s reasonable control (natural disaster, severe weather, government order, sudden serious illness, power outage, fire, flooding, **family medical emergency**, **pet medical emergency requiring immediate attention**), either party may cancel without penalty. The Client receives a full refund.

Documentation may be requested for repeated emergency claims to prevent abuse.

---

## 9. Refund Processing

Refunds are returned to the **original payment method** through Stripe. Processing typically takes **5–10 business days** to appear on the statement, depending on the Client’s bank or card issuer.

PureTask does **not** issue cash refunds, gift cards, or stored credit in lieu of refund to the original method, except where a Client expressly requests a PureTask service credit and PureTask agrees.

---

## 10. Disputes and Partial Refunds

If a Client is dissatisfied with completed work, they may file a **dispute** through the Platform within **24 hours** of completion. Disputes are handled under Section 7.4 of the Terms of Service. Resolution outcomes may include partial refunds, re-do bookings, or no action, at PureTask’s reasonable discretion based on the evidence.

---

## 11. Tip Refunds

Tips are paid post-completion at the Client’s election. Tips are **not refundable** once submitted, except:
- Where the Pro is determined to have committed material misconduct related to the same Booking;
- Where the tip was submitted in error and reported to PureTask within 24 hours.

---

## 12. State-Specific Notes

- **California**: All cancellation fees are disclosed in the booking flow before payment, consistent with **Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 17500.5**. Clients have all rights provided under California consumer protection law.
- **Texas**: Cancellation terms are governed by the booking contract and the **Texas Deceptive Trade Practices Act, Tex. Bus. & Com. Code Ch. 17**. Clients have the right to dispute charges through their card issuer.
- **Florida**: Cancellation terms are governed by the booking contract and the **Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act, Fla. Stat. Ch. 501**. Florida’s 3-day home-solicitation cancellation right (Fla. Stat. § 501.025) does not apply to PureTask Bookings, which are Client-initiated electronic transactions and not home-solicitation sales.
