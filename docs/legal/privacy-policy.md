## 2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in several categories:

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** name, email, phone number, password, role (Client or Cleaner).
- **Profile information:** photo, bio, languages, skills, service areas, availability.
- **Property and booking information:** addresses, access instructions, property type, pets, scope of work.
- **Payment information:** card details (collected and processed by our payment processor — we store only tokens and limited metadata such as last-4 digits and expiry), payout bank details for Cleaners.
- **Identity & trust information (Cleaners):** government ID, date of birth, Social Security Number (last 4) or Tax ID for IRS reporting, background-check authorization, certifications.
- **Communications:** in-app messages, dispute submissions, support tickets, reviews, ratings.
- **User-generated content:** before/after photos, notes, attachments.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device and usage data:** IP address, device type, OS, browser, app version, language, time zone, referrer, pages viewed, features used, crash logs, session duration.
- **Location data:** approximate location (from IP) and, with your permission, precise GPS location for arrival/departure check-ins (Cleaners) or for service-area matching.
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** see our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies).

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Identity verification & background check vendors** (e.g., for Cleaner onboarding).
- **Payment processors** (e.g., Stripe) — transaction status, risk signals.
- **Marketing & analytics providers**, **map and geocoding providers**, **fraud-prevention services**.
- **Referral sources** — when another User refers you to the Platform.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under **18 years of age**.

## 3. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to:

1. **Provide the Platform** — create accounts, match Clients with Cleaners, process bookings and payments, hold and release escrow Credits, deliver notifications.
2. **Verify identity, trust, and safety** — confirm Cleaner eligibility, perform background and watchlist screening, detect fraud, prevent abuse.
3. **Process payments and payouts** — charge cards, manage Credits, issue payouts and tax forms.
4. **Communicate** — send transactional messages (booking confirmations, receipts, dispute updates), service announcements, and (with consent where required) marketing.
5. **Personalize and improve** — tailor matching, recommendations, reliability scoring, and content.
6. **Analytics and research** — measure usage, debug, develop new features.
7. **Legal and compliance** — comply with laws, respond to legal process, enforce our Terms, protect rights, property, and safety.

### 3.1 Legal Bases (where applicable)
Where laws such as the GDPR apply, our legal bases are: **contract performance**, **legitimate interests** (e.g., fraud prevention, product improvement), **consent** (e.g., marketing emails, precise location), and **legal obligation**.

## 4. How We Share Personal Information

We share personal information with:

- **Other Users** — to enable a booking. Clients see Cleaner names, photos, ratings, and approximate service area. Cleaners see Client first name, booking address (only after assignment), and access notes. Reviews are visible to the User community.
- **Service providers and processors** acting on our behalf, including hosting (Supabase), payments (Stripe), identity verification, background checks, communications (email/SMS/push), analytics, customer support, and security vendors.
- **Legal and safety recipients** — law enforcement, regulators, and other parties when we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect rights, property, or safety.
- **Business transfers** — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.
- **With your consent** — for any other purpose disclosed to you.

We **do not sell** personal information for monetary consideration. We may "share" limited identifiers and online activity with advertising partners for cross-context behavioral advertising, which qualifies as a "sale" or "share" under CCPA/CPRA; you may opt out (see Section 8).

### 8.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)
You have the right to: (a) **know** what personal information we collect, use, disclose, sell, or share; (b) **delete** personal information, subject to exceptions; (c) **correct** inaccurate information; (d) **opt out** of "sale" or "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising; (e) **limit** use of sensitive personal information; and (f) **non-discrimination** for exercising your rights.

To exercise, email **otherpuretask@gmail.com** with the subject "**CCPA Request**" and your account email. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by 45 more if necessary). Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf with written authorization.

You may also click "**Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information**" in the website footer (where shown) and we honor recognized opt-out preference signals such as **Global Privacy Control (GPC)**.

### 8.3 Other U.S. State Rights
Residents of states with comparable laws (Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, etc.) have similar rights and may submit requests via the same channel.

### 8.4 EEA/UK Residents
You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests, and to withdraw consent at any time. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.
