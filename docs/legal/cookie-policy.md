# PureTask — Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** May 17, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how **Nathan Chiaratti, d/b/a PureTask** ("**PureTask**") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile applications (the "**Platform**"). It supplements our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Similar technologies include **local storage**, **session storage**, **pixels/web beacons**, **SDKs**, and **device identifiers**. We use these to make the Platform work, to remember your preferences, to keep you signed in, to measure performance, and to support limited advertising.

## 2. Categories of Cookies We Use

| Category | Purpose | Examples | Can you disable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strictly necessary** | Authentication, security, fraud prevention, load balancing, session continuity. | Auth tokens, CSRF tokens, session IDs. | No — the Platform will not function without these. |
| **Functional** | Remember preferences such as language, theme, last-used filters, dismissed banners. | UI preferences, "remember me." | Yes, via browser settings (may degrade experience). |
| **Performance / analytics** | Measure usage, debug errors, understand funnels and feature adoption. | Lovable Analytics, error tracking. | Yes, via our cookie banner or by enabling **Global Privacy Control (GPC)**. |
| **Advertising / cross-context** | Limited marketing measurement and (where used) personalized advertising on third-party sites. | Conversion pixels, retargeting tags. | Yes — opt out via cookie banner, browser settings, or GPC. |

## 3. First-Party vs. Third-Party

- **First-party cookies** are set by puretask.co (and our subdomains).
- **Third-party cookies** are set by service providers such as Stripe (payment risk), Supabase (auth/session), analytics, and advertising vendors.

## 4. Mobile SDKs and Push Tokens

Our mobile app uses SDKs (e.g., OneSignal for push notifications) that may store identifiers on your device. You can manage push notifications in your device settings.

## 5. Your Choices

- **Cookie banner.** Where applicable (e.g., for visitors in California, the EEA, the UK, and other regulated regions), we present a banner allowing you to accept, reject, or customize non-essential cookies.
- **Browser controls.** Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Note that disabling necessary cookies may break the Platform.
- **Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control.** We honor the GPC signal as an opt-out of "sale" and "sharing" under CCPA/CPRA.
- **Mobile.** Use your OS privacy settings (Limit Ad Tracking on iOS; reset advertising ID on Android).
